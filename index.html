The application notes in the 7805 datasheet state you should use capacitors on the input and output sides of the regulator. Also, depending on the amount of current required by the PSP – it may be wise to attach a heatsink to the 7805. For battery powered applications – it may be a better idea to use an LM2940-5 regulator because it requires less input voltage than the 7805 (5.75 V compared to 7 V) and has built-in thermal overload protection.
